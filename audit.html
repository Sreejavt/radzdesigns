How We Diagnose Conversion/Funnel Leaks

Stripe Audit — 5-Card Demo Breakdown (Pricing Architecture Teardown)

Information Architecture Failure

What Broke

Stripe's pricing page collapses multiple hierarchies—pricing tiers, features, and modular products—into a single cognitive layer.

Why It Matters

Overlapping navigation and hidden information create clarity anxiety, stall time-to-value (TTV), and break the zero-friction path for both SMB and Enterprise users.

Value Prioritization Flaw

What Broke

High-value signals for the Custom (Enterprise) tier are buried under jargon, feature dumps, and unclear cost framing.

Why It Matters

Enterprise buyers experience FUD around pricing transparency, hidden costs, and ROI—leading to delayed decisions or competitor deflection.

Decision Journey Failures

What Broke

Stripe assumes prior product knowledge and provides no guidance system to help users choose the right plan.

Why It Matters

Fragmented social proof and lack of hand-holding slow onboarding momentum, reduce conversion velocity, and increase funnel abandonment.

The Structural Fix

What Changed

Rebuilt pricing architecture by separating concerns:

  • Clear Standard vs Custom tier comparison
  • Core features decoupled from pricing
  • Device-specific layouts to reduce cognitive load
  • Strategic placement of quantified social proof
Why It Works

Restores clarity, accelerates time-to-value, and re-establishes a zero-friction decision path—especially for enterprise buyers.

Expected Outcomes

Projected Impact
  • 20-30% increase in high-value (Custom tier) inquiries
  • Reduced bounce from pricing clarity and cognitive load mitigation
  • Recovery of a multi-million-dollar enterprise ROI leak
Why This Matters

This isn't visual polish—it's revenue architecture. Structural clarity directly compounds enterprise conversion yield.

Notion Audit — 5-Card Demo Breakdown
(CTA Architecture And Conversion Flow Teardown)

Information Architecture Failure

What Broke

Notion collapses core product actions, secondary products, and ecosystem add-ons into a single undifferentiated flow.

Why It Matters

Users can't clearly distinguish what's core vs optional, creating clarity anxiety and slowing comprehension at the moment decisions should accelerate.

CTA Fragmentation & Conversion Noise

What Broke

Over 20 competing CTAs—duplicate buttons, arrows, links, and platform-specific prompts—fight for attention across the page.

Why It Matters

Excessive choice creates decision paralysis, signals low confidence in the primary offer, and stalls conversion velocity.

Onboarding Friction & TTV Stall

What Broke

High-impact value moments (like the $4,080 annual savings figure) are followed by interruptions, feature overload, and unnecessary navigation.

Why It Matters

Momentum breaks at peak intent, delaying time-to-value and increasing the probability of funnel abandonment.

The Structural Fix

What Changed

CTA architecture was consolidated into a clear hierarchy:

  • One low-friction primary action
  • Outcome-driven feature grouping
  • Static, interruption-free social proof
  • Clear separation of core product vs ecosystem add-ons
Why It Works

Reduces cognitive load, restores a zero-friction path, and guides users from interest to action without interruption.

Expected Outcomes

Projected Impact
  • ~15% lift in trial conversions
  • ~25% reduction in bounce rates
  • Faster onboarding momentum and reduced decision fatigue
Why This Matters

This isn't UI cleanup — it's conversion architecture.Structural clarity compounds sign-ups by removing friction at every decision point.

How It Works

Our Workflow

  1. You submit your funnel: Share your website or funnel URL through our contact form.
  2. We conduct the audit: We apply our 14-point framework to diagnose information architecture flaws, UX friction, message misalignment, CTA issues, and trust gaps across your funnel.
  3. You receive the breakdown: A detailed audit report with specific findings, prioritized fixes, and actionable recommendations—delivered as a PDF within 5-7 business days.
  4. Optional-Implementation Support: If you want us to execute the fixes, we can discuss funnel redesign or ongoing optimization.

These audits are diagnostic tools, not guarantees of specific conversion lifts.Projected outcomes are based on industry benchmarks and behavioral analysis. Actual results depend on implementation quality, traffic volume, and market conditions. We identify what's broken and how to fix it; execution and testing determine the final impact.
